01 / Manifesto
Why now
A note from the
founder.

Everyone is talking about AI in quality engineering. Few are showing the proof.

We've watched the hype cycle. We've sat through the same demos. We know what an AI-generated test looks like — and we know what actually shipped to production and held up.

Elevate QA exists for the second conversation. The harder one. The one where engineers, leaders, and practitioners put real work on the table — what they tried, what broke, what changed the math.

No vendor pitches. No abstract theory. The proof of value, or it didn't happen.

02 / The Map

Where is your team on the AI-led QE curve?

STAGE 01FOUNDATION
Manual-first

Test cases authored by hand. Automation islands. AI is "interesting," not yet operational.

~ 25% of orgs surveyed
STAGE 02EXPLORATORY
Assisted

AI helps generate test cases and data. Engineers stay in the loop. Early wins, mixed signals.

~ 50% of orgs surveyed
STAGE 03OPERATIONAL
Augmented

Self-healing automation, intelligent triage, AI-driven coverage gap analysis. Measurable lift.

~ 18% of orgs surveyed
STAGE 04FRONTIER
Autonomous

Quality agents reason about risk, prioritize, and adapt. Humans set strategy. The future, already here in pockets.

~ 7% of orgs surveyed
